Attention Is All You Need

注意力机制就是你所需要的一切
Ashish Vaswani, Noam Shazeer, Niki Parmar, Jakob Uszkoreit, Llion Jones, Aidan N. Gomez, Lukasz Kaiser, Illia Polosukhin

Abstract

摘要

The dominant sequence transduction models are based on complex recurrent or convolutional neural networks that include an encoder and a decoder. The best performing models also connect the encoder and decoder through an attention mechanism.

主流的序列转导模型基于复杂的循环或卷积神经网络，通常包含编码器与解码器。表现最好的模型还会通过注意力机制连接编码器与解码器。

We propose a new simple network architecture, the Transformer, based solely on attention mechanisms, dispensing with recurrence and convolutions entirely.

我们提出一种全新的简单网络架构Transformer，它完全基于注意力机制，彻底不再依赖循环与卷积结构。

1 Introduction

1 引言

Recurrent neural networks, long short-term memory and gated recurrent neural networks in particular, have been firmly established as state of the art approaches in sequence modeling and transduction problems such as language modeling and machine translation.

循环神经网络，尤其是长短期记忆网络与门控循环网络，已成为序列建模与转导任务（如语言建模和机器翻译）中的主流方法。

Attention mechanisms have become an integral part of compelling sequence modeling and transduction models in various tasks, allowing modeling of dependencies without regard to their distance in the input or output sequences. In all cases, the calculation can be described as mapping a query and a set of key-value pairs to an output, where the output is computed as a weighted sum of the values.

注意力机制已成为多种序列建模与转导模型的核心组件，能够建模输入/输出序列中任意距离的依赖关系。其计算可描述为：将查询（query）与一组键值对（key-value）映射为输出，输出是值的加权和。
